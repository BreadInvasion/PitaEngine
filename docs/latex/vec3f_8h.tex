Returns the cross product of a and b. 

The cross product of two vectors is a vector that is orthogonal to the plane created by the two -\/ that is, it is perpendicular to both a and b. This is relevant in a variety of operations relating to vectors and planes -\/ it is important to note, though, that the direction of the vector created by this process {\itshape is} predictable (since its inverse is also orthogonal to the plane), using the right hand rule.

Since we\textquotesingle{}re working linearly, the individual matching components can be interpolated by the same constant factor.
